Objectives

As the Covid-19 virus overtook the world, many people found themselves worried and fearful for their and their loved ones' lives. The race for a vaccine to alleviate the illness began immediately, and people anxiously waited to get their hands on one. At the time that this project was in the works, there were not enough vaccines to go around. Our team wanted to focus on providing vaccine information to those in the state of Georgia where we live as various states provide such a feature that Georgia was, at the time, lacking. Our app seeks to ease the process of determining whether vaccines are available in the vicinity of people and where they can register to get one.

Research

User Research

We started researching users that this app would benefit, namely anyone who would be eligible to receive a Covid-19 vaccine within the next few months. Between a Google Form survey, four user interviews, and information gathered from social media and the news, we quickly pulled together features that people were looking for to alleviate the lack of communication about vaccines. In a perfect world, users wanted an app that would state who was eligible for the vaccine, with information about the different vaccines offered (Pfizer, Moderna, Johnson & Johnson), and ways to search for and be notified of vaccine availability at nearby venues. This was extremely important at the time because, according to the 21 surveys we received, over 90% of these users had not been inoculated against the virus by means of a vaccine yet.
